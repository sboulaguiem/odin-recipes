Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is very simple and delicious. You will need only 4 ingredients and it will take you 15 mn to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta sheets
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground meat

Steps

  1. Take all the ingredients
  2. mix them into a bowl
  3. Layer that with the pasta sheets
  4. Put them in the oven
